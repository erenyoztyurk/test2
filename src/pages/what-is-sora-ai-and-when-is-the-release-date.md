# What is Sora AI

## Overview
Sora AI is an artificial intelligence platform developed by Google, designed to enhance and simplify AI development for individuals and organizations. It serves as a unified interface for various AI services, providing a seamless experience for building, deploying, and managing AI models.

## Key Features
Sora AI offers several key features that make it an attractive solution for AI development:

### 1. Unified Interface
Sora AI provides a single interface for accessing various AI services, eliminating the need to navigate multiple platforms.

### 2. Model Development
The platform offers a range of tools and frameworks for building, training, and deploying AI models, including support for popular frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch.

### 3. Data Management
Sora AI provides a robust data management system, enabling users to easily manage, label, and preprocess data for AI model training.

### 4. Model Deployment
The platform allows users to deploy AI models in various environments, including on-premises, cloud, and edge devices.
